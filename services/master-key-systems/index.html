Our Master Key Services

Restricted Keyways Patent-protected blanks that prevent unauthorized duplication.

Existing Lock Integration Re-pin compatible locks into a unified system.

How Master Key Systems Work

  • Owner/Grand Master Opens every door in the facility
  • Department Masters Opens all doors within a specific zone
  • Individual Keys Opens only the assigned office or area

Benefits for Your Business

  • One Key for Managers Eliminate bulky keyrings
  • Controlled Duplication Know exactly how many keys exist
  • Employee Zones Staff only access their authorized areas
  • Scalable Design System grows with your business

Ideal For

Property management, apartment buildings, office complexes, medical facilities, schools, hotels, and multi-tenant commercial buildings.
